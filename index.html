Casl Blue

UPDATE: sign-up for the newsletter on casl.blue

Currently in development

The motivation behind Casl Media, Casl Blue is a music streaming service that is focused on filling a niche left vacant since modern copyright legislation was passed. There is still a space to be filled by some communication medium which benefits both consumers and producers of music. As avid consumers we have watched the community attempt to solve this problem, we just want to polish those attempts with a little engineering. See casl.blue for more information and to get involved.

Open Epistemology

UPDATE: the project has started and is hosted on GitHub

In the idea stage

Hopefully this project will blossom into the majority of my life's work. Knowledge is being communicated in an abundance of new forms thanks to the internet but I'm yet to be convinced that any one solution is the right one. Exploring the different mediums available is entertaining, some have ideas I will use, but I have yet to find one based on the foundations I believe are necessary.

The current clay model is focused on “the mathematical explanation” as the core. With some form of mapping on top as the aesthetics. About

I'm an engineer.

I enjoy discussing problems, I see greater value in thinking about them, but my passion is solving them.

Current topics of interest are themed "how things connect".
Philosophy (epistemology), mathematics (graph theory), physics (external reality hypothesis), cognition(knowledge mapping), complexity(complex systems), among other buzzwords.

Unfortunately I'm an admitted book addict, I spend too much time researching them and not enough time reading them. I want to build an addiction aiding tool, see The Typographic Mind under Projects.
