Writing Lab last updated May 23rd, 2015

The writing lab is my attempt to put something back out into the world.

I'm not sure what the final delivery method will be, which is why it's a lab. It will likely change between topics and with time.

I do know some of my ideas yearn to be free for interpretation. They lust for crisitism and have an ambition to spark thought in other freethinking individuals.

I would like to do some long-form writing, possibly full-time. I have some drafts (mostly outlines) in progress. If you're someone who can help me finish one of these attempts please reach out at pubic@jeclevenger.com.
